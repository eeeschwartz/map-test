Lexington-Fayette Urban County Government Traffic Cameras

The LFUCG Traffic Website is provided to help motorists make more informed travel decisions when traveling Lexington’s roadways. Users can view live streaming video in real time from the LFUCG’s network of Closed Circuit Television (CCTV) cameras. Cameras are accessible via a list view and Google-based dynamic map.

Areas

An expandable menu is located on the left side of the page with a list of available cameras. Click the + next to Areas to expand the list. A list of areas with CCTV cameras will be displayed. When you click on an area, the map will automatically zoom to that area on the map. A camera icon appears on the map wherever there is a CCTV camera. Click on a camera icon to access video, and a pop up will appear. Then, click anywhere on the camera snapshot to play live streaming video from the camera. You can also click anywhere to pause the video.

Cameras

Click the + next to Cameras to view a list of all cameras. A snapshot and description of each camera will be displayed. Click on the camera snapshot. The map will automatically zoom to the camera icon, and the pop up will appear. Click anywhere on the camera snapshot to play live streaming video from the camera. You can also click anywhere to pause the video.

Other Notes

You can also use the +/- buttons at the bottom right of the map to manually zoom into a specific area or camera. Only one camera can be accessed at a time. Each camera has a unique URL, which can be shared if quick and direct access is needed.

Disclaimer: Streaming video is at no time being recorded.
